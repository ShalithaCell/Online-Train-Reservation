Frequently Asked Questions


1) How are sleeping compartments kept secure?

Night trains have conductors on duty all through the train ride. What's more, sleeping compartments have bolts on the entryways that you can control. Moreover, outside of these compartments are under high security camera surveillance.

2) Can I bring food and drinks on board?

Obviously you can! Eating and drinking are allowed on every train. You will also have the choice to purchase food and drinks if the train you're going on has any canteen in it.

3) How much luggage can I take on the train?

Actually, you are not restricted to bring baggage. However, remember that you'll have to bring your baggage through the train station and onto the train. Once ready you'll have to discover a spot to keep your baggage. Little and medium measured packs regularly fit on racks situated over the seats. Bigger bags and suitcases can be put away in gear racks found close to the train entryway entrance. Know that you are responsible your baggage. The railroads accept no responsibility in case of lost of your valuables or belongings.

4) Are there smoking and non-smoking sections on trains?

Smoking isn't allowed on any trains in Great Britain, Denmark, Ireland, Benelux (Belgium, Netherlands and Luxembourg), France, Hungary, Spain, Turkey, Italy, Switzerland, Austria, Germany, Norway, Sweden, or Poland.

5) Is there WiFi onboard?

Most of the 1st class trains have Free WiFi. But only few 2nd class trains have got WIFI. Moreover 2nd class train WiFi is also not for free, you have to pay a little fee for it. Here is a brief list of trains offering WiFi:
> openTrainWIFI
> ExpressWIFI
> StarWIFI
> freeWIFI4U

6) Can I travel with my pets?

Yes, you can travel with small pets such as dogs, cats, and some domestic birds. But the maximum weight of 10Kg. Not only that you need to acquire a legal “Pet Pass” from the station which you gets up to the train. Each pet should be kept locked inside the cage for all the time. If you have any questions regarding this, please do not hesitate to contact us.

7) I didn’t receive any tickets but I was still charged!

There may be delay in your bank system but do not worry you will receive a email with your train ticket.

8) What if I have forgotten or don't know my password?
