About BOOkit

Sri Lanka consist of around 396 trains with 67 Long-Distance and 16 Intercity trains and convoys about 3.72 million travels everyday. In Sri Lanka there are 1561km of rail tracks, 72 locomotives, 78 power sets, 565 carrages and the signaling systems. By now, it has a work force of 17,634.

There are several train types. They are,
1) Intercity expresss : among the quickest trains, with few stops (need to get a specific ticket for these trains, moreover ticket cost is more higher than the ordinary tickets)
2) Night Mail : Cargo transport night-time expresss trains.
3) Express : Link Colombo and other important and most crowded transport cities.
4) Rural : General trains which stops at almost every station.
